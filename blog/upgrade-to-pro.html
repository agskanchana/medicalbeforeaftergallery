3. Image Watermarking: Protect Your Valuable Assets

Your before-after images represent years of expertise and countless hours of work. Unfortunately, image theft is rampant in the medical aesthetics industry. Competitors and unethical practitioners often steal impressive results to misrepresent their own capabilities.

MBA Gallery Pro's watermarking feature offers comprehensive protection:

  • Custom Logo Watermarks: Add your practice logo to every image
  • Text Watermarks: Include practice name, website, or copyright notice
  • Position Control: Place watermarks strategically without obscuring results
  • Opacity Adjustment: Balance visibility with image quality
  • Batch Application: Apply watermarks to all existing images at once

Legal Protection

Watermarked images provide legal evidence of ownership if images are stolen. Several MBA Gallery Pro users have successfully pursued copyright claims thanks to their watermarked galleries.

4. Sensitive Content Warning: HIPAA-Friendly Privacy Protection

One of the most requested Pro features, sensitive content warnings allow you to showcase graphic or intimate procedures while respecting viewer sensitivities and maintaining HIPAA compliance:

  • Customizable Warning Messages: Tailor messages to your practice and procedure types
  • Blur Effect: Images remain blurred until viewer acknowledges warning
  • Age Verification: Optional age gate for particularly sensitive content
  • Per-Case Configuration: Apply warnings only where needed

This feature is particularly valuable for:

  • Plastic surgery involving intimate areas
  • Severe dermatological conditions
  • Reconstructive surgery after trauma
  • Any procedure with graphic pre-treatment images

This comprehensive documentation builds trust by demonstrating transparency and thoroughness in your patient care.
